# QuickBooks EU Chart of accounts 2026
2026 QuickBooks Online EU Chart of Accounts (COA) - Types & Details

1. Introduction

This repository provides a comprehensive and structured list of the QuickBooks Online EU Chart of Accounts (2026 Edition). This project consolidates the Section, Account Type, and Detail Type mapping required for accurate EU financial reporting, serving as a definitive resource for finance professionals and business owners.

2. Strategic Value

Accuracy: Directly aligned with the 2026 EU localization standards for QuickBooks Online.

Versatility: Provided in multiple formats to suit various professional needs, from manual review to bulk data processing.

Localization: Optimized for EU entities managing VAT, multi-currency, and complex logistics structures.

3. Data Structure

The documentation covers the five essential financial categories:

Assets: Current, Fixed, and Non-Current assets including specific EU tax deferrals.

Liabilities: Detailed breakdown of current and long-term obligations.

Equity: Owner's equity and share capital structures.

Income: Operating and non-operating revenue types.

Expenses: Comprehensive list including Cost of Sales (COS) and Administrative expenses.

4. Available Formats

To ensure maximum accessibility, the data is provided in the following formats:

QBO_EU_COA_2026.xlsx: Best for active financial modeling and filtering.

QBO_EU_COA_2026.csv: Ideal for bulk importing into accounting software.

QBO_EU_COA_2026.pdf: Formatted for easy printing and offline reference.

QBO_EU_COA_2026.txt: Simple raw text for quick searches or custom scripting.

QBO_EU_COA_2026.jpg: Visual reference for quick viewing on mobile devices or tablets.

5. Implementation

When setting up your QuickBooks Online environment, match the Account Type and Detail Type from these files to ensure your Balance Sheet and Profit & Loss statements are structured correctly for EU compliance.
